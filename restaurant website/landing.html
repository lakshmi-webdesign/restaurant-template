ABOUT US

Elegance. Energy.

Innovation.

Nestled within one of Mumbai’s old textile mills, The ABC is an ingredient-driven restaurant.
The restaurant’s 14-course chef’s tasting menus - a first of its kind in India – marry tradition and innovation as the seasons change, so do the menus, defined by ingredients from the restaurant’s farm, foraging trips, and a network of local farmers. Each kitchen is led by a talented chef whose passion lies in creating uniquely rooted yet modern dishes.

With international outposts in some of the world’s most vibrant cities, The ABC's menu is a culinary balance of signature dishes available at any The ABC's location and dishes curated with local tastes and traditions in mind.
